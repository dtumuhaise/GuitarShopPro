About

_______


Inspiration for GuitarTechPro struck when I found myself repeatedly struggling to keep track of guitars undergoing repairs. The frustration of forgetting crucial details, such as the owner, arrival date, and repair requirements, sparked a desire to simplify the process. Driven by my passion for guitars and technology and as a portifolio project for ALX school, I embarked on creating GuitarTechProâ€”a solution to streamline guitar repair management.

Features

_______

Comprehensive Guitar Profile Management

Effortlessly create and manage profiles for each guitar in your repair shop. Capture important details such as the owner's name, arrival date, and repair requirements. Keep all the essential information organized and accessible in one place.
Progress Tracking and Task Management
 
Stay on top of every repair job with GuitarTechPro's progress tracking and task management feature. Easily track the status of each repair, log completed tasks, and set reminders for pending actions. Never miss a step and ensure efficient workflow management.

Repair Notes and Documentation

Allow repair technicians to add detailed notes and documentation about each repair job, including observations, repair steps, and recommendations.
